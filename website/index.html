FOUR WAYS TO CONNECT

Any link you have, Tarkk speaks it.

WiFi channel

One phone hotspots, everyone joins. Open-channel voice with VOX — hands stay on the handlebars.

Bluetooth pair

No network at all? Two phones link directly over Bluetooth — rock-solid Android-to-Android, with auto-reconnect when the link drops and a bounded queue so a slow link never piles up into lag.

Hotspot bridge

iPhone and Android, guaranteed. One Android phone raises a Wi-Fi hotspot; the iPhone joins by scanning a code — then it's full-quality voice over Wi-Fi.

Browser guest

Friends without the app join from Safari or Chrome — scan a QR or just send them the invite link. Works over the internet too, not just the same room. Still no server.

MUSIC CAST

One phone plays. Everyone rides to it.

Cast your phone's music straight into the channel. Your voice rides on top of the track, a mix slider keeps the balance right on every device — yours included — and stopping the cast can pause the track too.

UNDER THE HOOD

Built like a radio, engineered like software.

CODEC

Opus · 16 kHz mono

~20–30 kbps compressed — a tenth the size of raw PCM, with no perceptible loss.

VOICE CLARITY

AEC · NS · AGC

Echo cancellation, noise suppression and gain control run live on every capture session.

LATENCY

Configurable jitter buffer

Just enough buffering to stay smooth without ever feeling behind — self-corrects clock drift instead of letting delay quietly grow over a long ride. Adjustable in Settings.

ALWAYS ON

Foreground service

A wake lock and multicast lock keep the channel alive with the screen off.

RECONNECT

Exponential backoff

A liveness watchdog catches a link gone silent — not just closed — so a dead peer heals itself instead of needing a manual leave/rejoin. Retries from 4s up to 64s, resetting the instant the link returns.

TRANSPORT

UDP broadcast

Direct peer discovery on your LAN, with a unicast fallback for iOS.

AUDIO CUES

Eyes-free feedback

A distinct sound for PTT, peers, and reconnects — built for riding with the phone in a pocket.

FOUR WAYS TO CONNECT

Any link you have, Tark speaks it.

WiFi channel

One phone hotspots, everyone joins. Open-channel voice with VOX — hands stay on the handlebars.

Bluetooth pair

No network at all? Two phones link directly over Bluetooth — rock-solid Android-to-Android, with auto-reconnect when the link drops.

Hotspot bridge

iPhone and Android, guaranteed. One Android phone raises a Wi-Fi hotspot; the iPhone joins by scanning a code — then it's full-quality voice over Wi-Fi.

Browser guest

Friends without the app scan two QR codes and join from Safari or Chrome. Pure LAN WebRTC — still no server.
